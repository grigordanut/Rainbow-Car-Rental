Toyota RAV4

Rent this car
Toyota RAV4:

Its angled headlamps and sharply creased bodywork give the RAV4 an assertive presence, but don’t be fooled—under the skin, it’s a practical, comfortable, and easy-going use. A 176-hp 2.5-liter four and a six-speed automatic are standard with either front- or all-wheel drive. A hybrid, which returned an impressive 31 mpg in our tests, is also available and gets standard all-wheel drive. A 6.1-inch infotainment system is standard on LE, SE, and XLE trims; Limited models get a 7.0-inch unit.
